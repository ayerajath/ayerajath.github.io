Recently saw the news of **Zepto's founder Aadit mentioning that they are what Amazon was in the early 2000s.**

Just this statement got the folks on Twitter/X talking. Most of them criticizing Aadit for this statement. The first thing that people do is to criticize. Just irrational hate among people. 

All I have to say is that **setting high standards is a good thing.** This is something that will set apart anyone. Dreaming and setting high standards is what makes a company or oneself grow. 

Why would anyone want to be average? When you can be great, and everyone is great. It's just that we tend to underplay this. We forget about this. 

Not dreaming big is what kills a company. Not dreaming big is what kills an individual. Dream big, Do big, Do shit and success and learnings with follow. 
Am I doing this right? I do not know. But I just got to try it out. 

I have high hopes for Zepto, I know they will do great. Being the fastest growing company in India is no joke.
